How to Play
Controls
Feed Your Fish
Tap anywhere on the water to drop food. Fish will swim towards it and eat if they are hungry.
Autofeed
Enable autofeed to automatically drop food for your fish so they always stay well-fed.
Spirit Mode
Enable spirit mode then tap on fish to hear them speak and see their name, age, and hunger level.
Spirit Memories
Keep track of births and deaths in your aquarium. Each fish has a unique name and story.
Shop
Earn spirit orbs by feeding your fish. Spend your orbs in the shop to unlock new species.
Fish Behavior
Schooling
Fish of the same species swim together in schools. The oldest fish will normally lead the group.
Breeding
Well-fed adult fish of the same species may have babies when they spend time together.
Predators
Some fish are predators and will hunt smaller fish. Add predators to your aquarium at your own risk!
Day & Night
Most fish slow down at night, but predators keep hunting. Adjust the day/night cycle speed in settings.
Growth
Baby fish start small and grow by eating. Babies have a bigger appetite than adults!
